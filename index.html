Google Review Link Generator

Use our free tool to easily find your business and generate your unique Google Review link.
Then share the short URL with your customers and grow your Google reviews.

To generate your review link:

1. Type your business name, city, and zip/postal code in the “Enter a Business” field.
2. Your review link will appear under the map.
3. Copy the URL to your clipboard and shorten it using a tool like bitly.
4. Share the link with customers in emails, text messages, or even on printouts, and ask them to leave you a review on Google!
